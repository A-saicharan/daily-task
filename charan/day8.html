Climate change is not a fixed number of "types," but it is commonly explained in 5 major categories based on its impacts.

See More

1. Global Warming

  • Increase in the Earth's average temperature.
  • caused by greenhouse gas emissions.

2. Changes in Rainfall Patterns

  1. Some regions receive heavier rainfall and floods.
  2. Other regions experience droughts and water shortages.

3. Sea Level Rise

  • Glaciers and polar ice melt.
  • Warmer oceans expand, causing sea levels to rise

4. Melting Ice and Glaciers

  1. Ice sheets and glaciers shrink.
  2. This affects freshwater supplies and wildlife habitats

5. Extreme Weather Events

  • More frequent and intense:
    1. Heatwaves
    2. Floods
    3. Droughts
    4. Cyclones and hurricanes
